Answer the questions related to a new situation defined as: The supervisor of the manufacturing process prefers to have a RED LED to turn ON when either tank A or tank B or Both tank levels drops below ¼ full-level.
Q1. What modification would be required in the external circuit (limiting resistor & RED LED) connected to the output of NAND gate?
A  The cathode of RED LED should be connected to + 5 V.
B  The cathode of RED LED should be connected to ground.

Ans is B


Q2. If external connections are done according to the solution of Q.1; then we refer to the IC as _____________ current.
A  Sinking
B  Sourcing
C  Limiting
D  None of these

Ans is B


Q3. When is the output of NAND gate LOW?
A  When tank A is empty
B  When tank B is empty
C  When tanks A & B both are empty.
D  All of these

Ans is C


Q4. In this situation which gate could be used instead of NAND gate?
A  AND
B  OR
C  XOR
D  XNOR

Ans is B


Q5. Do these logic gates have same truth table?

A  Yes
B  NO

Ans is A
