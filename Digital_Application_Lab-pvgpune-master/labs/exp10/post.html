Q1. If the switch SW0 is connected to ground and one of the door is left open, the alarm_________.
A  Sounds
B  Does not sound
C  Burns
D  None of these

Ans is A


Q2. If the switch SW0 is on position ‘a’, and the doors 3 and 4 are opened, the alarm ___________.
A  Sounds
B  Does not sound
C  Burns
D  None of these

Ans is B


Q3. To provide extra current to drive the alarm device, a __________ is used in the circuit:
A  OR gate
B  NOR gate
C  Buffer
D  Bubbled NAND

Ans is C
